The three systems

One answer, triangulated across 2,500 years of wisdom

α

Pythagorean

The Western system, originating with Pythagoras (c. 570 BC). Calculates life path, expression, soul urge, and personality numbers by converting letters A–Z to digits 1–9.

ܐ

Chaldean

Ancient Babylonian origin, c. 4,000 years old. Considered the most mystically accurate by Cheiro — uses a different letter-number mapping and reveals the karmic signature in your name.

ॐ

Vedic (Ankashastra)

Rooted in the Vedas and Hindu astrology. Your Moolank (psychic number) and Bhagyank (destiny number) link to the nine ruling planets — Sun, Moon, Jupiter, Rahu, Mercury, Venus, Ketu, Saturn, Mars.

☯

The 3×3 magic square from ancient China. Plots your birth date on a grid to reveal missing numbers, planes of influence, and life lessons.

Questions

Things readers ask first

Is numerology a science?
+
No — numerology is a symbolic system, not a scientific one. It's a framework for reflection, pattern recognition, and self-understanding, much like the I Ching or tarot. Use it as a lens for introspection, not as a predictor of concrete events.
Which system should I trust — Pythagorean, Chaldean, or Vedic?
+
Each answers different questions. Pythagorean is best for personality and expression (the "how" you show up). Chaldean is considered most accurate for name vibration and karmic signature. Vedic/Ankashastra links numbers to ruling planets — best for remedies and auspicious timing. Our synthesis view shows where all three agree, which is the most reliable signal.
Do I need to use my full name?
+
For the most accurate reading, yes — use the full name given at birth (as on your birth certificate). The name you were given carries your soul's original frequency. You can also calculate against your current name to see how much your identity has shifted.
What are master numbers (11, 22, 33)?
+
Master numbers carry heightened vibrations and are never reduced to single digits. 11 is the Illuminator (intuitive insight). 22 is the Master Builder (manifestation). 33 is the Master Teacher (compassionate leadership). If your life path or destiny number is a master number, you have both the gift and the burden of its elevated energy.
Can I change my name to improve my numerology?
+
Many people do — name correction is a classical Vedic remedy. If your expression number clashes with your life path, adjusting the spelling (adding or removing a letter) can bring them into harmony. Our name correction tool suggests up to 12 ranked variants. That said: legal name changes are a big commitment. Most people simply adopt the tuned spelling for signatures, business, and branding.
Is my data safe?
+
Yes. Calculations happen in your browser — your name and date of birth don't leave your device for the free calculator. For paid accounts, we store only the minimum needed (mobile number, email, subscription status). No data is shared with third parties. See our privacy policy.
