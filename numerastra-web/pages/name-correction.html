How it works

Small spellings, large vibrations

Every letter has a number. In the Chaldean system, A is 1, B is 2, C is 3 — but with intentional gaps (no 9 in Chaldean). Your complete name reduces to a single number. That number is your name vibration.

Your psychic number comes from your birth date's day-of-month. It's fixed at birth — unchangeable. Your name vibration can be tuned to match it, and that's where remedies come in.

A name in harmony means you introduce yourself with the same vibration your soul carries. Doors open easier. A clashing name means you're saying one thing with your words and another with your frequency — a subtle friction that compounds over years.

The remedy is small, not dramatic: add an H, remove a Y, double an N. These adjustments shift the total name number without changing who you are. Priya becomes Priyaa. Rahul becomes Rahool. Whether you adopt the change legally, professionally, or just on your letterhead — the energetic impact begins the day you sign your new name.
